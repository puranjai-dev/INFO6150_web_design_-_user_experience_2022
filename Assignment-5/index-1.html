Marketplace Features

Here's how NU Moves makes buying and selling goods a unique experience!

Chat

Live Chat on a product listing enables buyers and sellers to connect in realtime

Exclusive for Huskies

To facilitate maximum trust between the parties, NU MOVES is exclusively available for Northeastern University students only

Verified Members

Say goodbye to scammers, spammers and bots. NU MOVES requires users to login via northeastern email address and upload their Husky ID

Zero Commission

NU MOVES aims to operate on a zero commission model and won't charge a dime for any product bought, sold or rented

Personlized Dashboard

Users get access to a personalized dashboard to keep tabs on their purchases, listings and other relevant stats.

Location Filter

Set location filter to view listings from the specified radius distance.

How It Works?

Few simple steps to make moving in and moving out a smoother experience.

Create and Publish Product Listing

Answer simple questions about the product and make a compelling & comprehensive listing to attract qualified buyers.

Listing pushed for reach

NU MOVES will run the listing through a proprietary algorithm to increase the exposure among qualified buyers based on location and other parameters.

Connect with potential buyers

Chat with potential buyers and get the best deal for your goods.

NU MOVES - A Non-profit mission for Huskies on the move

NU MOVES aims to alleviate the hassle Huskies face every year while moving in and out of their accommodation. It provides a safe and reliable platform for a close-knit community of Northeastern Unioversity students to buy, sell & rent stuff among themselves.
